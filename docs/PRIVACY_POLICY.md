## Our Commitment to Privacy

Joystick Launcher is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe that your data belongs to you, and we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Information We DO NOT Collect

Joystick Launcher **DOES NOT**:

## Information That Stays on Your Device

The following data is accessed locally on your device and **NEVER leaves your device**:

### 1. Installed Applications List
- **Purpose**: To display all your installed apps in the app drawer
- **Scope**: Package names, app names, and app icons
- **Storage**: Local device memory only
- **Sharing**: Never shared or transmitted

### 2. Notifications
- **Purpose**: To display notifications in the notification shade
- **Scope**: Notification title, content, timestamp, and source app
- **Storage**: Temporary local memory, cleared when dismissed
- **Sharing**: Never shared or transmitted
- **Access**: Only you can see your notifications

### 3. System Settings
- **Purpose**: To check system theme (dark/light mode) and time
- **Scope**: Display settings and system clock
- **Storage**: Not stored, only queried when needed
- **Sharing**: Never shared or transmitted

## Permissions Explained

Joystick Launcher requires the following permissions to function:

### 1. QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
- **Purpose**: To list and display all installed applications
- **Usage**: Required to show apps in the app drawer
- **Privacy**: App list never leaves your device

### 2. BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE
- **Purpose**: To access and display notifications in the launcher
- **Usage**: Shows notifications in the notification shade
- **Privacy**: Notification content is never stored permanently or shared

### 3. ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY
- **Purpose**: To read notification settings
- **Usage**: Ensures proper notification display
- **Privacy**: Only reads settings, doesn't modify or share them

### 4. POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)
- **Purpose**: To display system notifications
- **Usage**: Shows notification updates
- **Privacy**: Standard Android permission, no data collection

### 5. VIBRATE
- **Purpose**: To provide haptic feedback during joystick use
- **Usage**: Vibrates when you cross joystick regions
- **Privacy**: No data associated, purely functional

## Data Storage

- **Stored locally** on your device

- **Not synchronized** across devices
- **Deleted** when you uninstall the app

If you wish to revoke any permissions:

## Uninstallation

To completely remove all app data:
1. Set a different launcher as default
2. Go to Settings → Apps → Joystick Launcher
3. Tap "Uninstall"
4. All app data will be permanently deleted from your device
