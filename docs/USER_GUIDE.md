# Joystick Launcher - User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to Joystick Launcher! This guide will help you understand and use all the features of this unique gesture-based launcher.

## Basic Navigation

### Home Screen

The home screen is your starting point and features:
- **Clock Display**: Large, easy-to-read time in the center
- **Date**: Current date displayed at the top
- **Quick Access**: Tap and hold to access different screens
- **Minimalist Design**: No clutter, just what you need

### Opening the App Drawer

There are multiple ways to access your apps:

1. **Swipe Up**: From the bottom of the screen
2. **Swipe Gesture**: Use the gesture controls
3. **Joystick**: Use the joystick interface for quick app access

### Using the Joystick

The joystick is the heart of this launcher:

1. **Appearance**: The joystick appears as a circular interface with your quick apps
2. **Dragging**: Touch and drag the joystick to move through apps
3. **Selection**: Apps are highlighted as you move over them
4. **Launch**: Release on an app to launch it
5. **Haptic Feedback**: Feel vibrations as you move between apps

#### Joystick Features:
- **Quick Apps**: Your most used apps arranged in a circle
- **Smooth Animation**: Fluid transitions and movements
- **Visual Feedback**: Clear indication of selected app
- **Region Boundaries**: Distinct zones for each app

## Managing Apps

### Browsing All Apps

1. Open the app drawer
2. Scroll through the alphabetically sorted list
3. All installed apps are displayed with their icons
4. Tap any app icon to launch it

### Launching Apps

- **From Joystick**: Drag to the app and release
- **From Drawer**: Tap the app icon
- **From Quick Access**: If configured, direct launch

## Notifications

### Viewing Notifications

1. **Swipe Down**: Pull down from the top of the screen
2. **Notification Shade**: All notifications appear in a list
3. **Notification Content**: 
   - App icon
   - Title
   - Message content
   - Time received

### Managing Notifications

- **Open Notification**: Tap on a notification to open the associated app
- **Dismiss**: Swipe or tap the dismiss button
- **Clear All**: Option to clear all notifications at once

### Notification Settings

To ensure you receive notifications:
1. Grant notification access permission when prompted
2. Keep the app battery optimization off
3. Check notification settings in Android settings

## Gestures & Controls

### Supported Gestures

1. **Tap**: Single tap to select/activate
2. **Long Press**: Access secondary screen
3. **Swipe Up**: Open app drawer
4. **Swipe Down**: Open notification shade
5. **Drag**: Use the joystick interface

### Tips for Better Gesture Control

- Use smooth, deliberate movements
- Don't move too quickly
- Wait for haptic feedback confirmation
- Practice the joystick for better accuracy

## Troubleshooting

### Joystick Not Responding
- Try tapping and holding for a moment
- Ensure touch sensitivity is normal
- Restart the launcher app

### Apps Not Showing in Drawer
- Check if notification listener permission is granted
- Verify query all packages permission
- Reinstall the app if issue persists

### Notifications Not Appearing
- Grant notification access in settings
- Disable battery optimization
- Restart device

### Launcher Not Set as Default
- Press Home button
- Select Joystick Launcher
- Tap "Always"

### Performance Issues
- Clear app cache
- Reduce number of quick apps
- Close background apps
- Restart device
